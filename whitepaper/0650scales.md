### Conclusion on Scalability

The Per-Content $\text{DAO}$'s design transforms the static, fixed-cost burden of content management into a dynamic, economically sustainable utility. By externalizing the governance structure into the **Income Distribution Ratio**, the system manages complexity through definition rather than through adding complex smart contract layers. This flexibility ensures $\text{IPFS-Sats}$ can host and govern any digital asset, guaranteeing its long-term persistence and relevance, from a musician's song to a government's economic data standard.
