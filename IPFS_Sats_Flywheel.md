# IPFS Sats: Unified Ecosystem Launch Strategy

## Executive Summary: The Universal Participation Model

The IPFS Sats protocol is a fundamental infrastructure layer designed to enable **100% participation** in the decentralized economy. It establishes a secure, permanent content layer (IPFS) and seamlessly integrates it with a native, instant payment rail (Bitcoin Sats via Lightning).

Our strategy is to simultaneously onboard six interdependent stakeholder groups who form a self-sustaining economic loop. The core vision is to build a system that scales from a single mobile device generating passive income to massive institutional liquidity providers, ensuring that every participant, regardless of size, is financially rewarded for their contribution to the network’s utility.

***

## The IPFS Sats Flywheel

This strategy is designed to create a powerful feedback loop:

1.  **Investors** fund the **Archival DAO**.
2.  The DAO creates high-value contracts that pay **Hosts** for storage/routing, generating **Yield for LYW Providers**.
3.  **Core Protocol Devs** build the robust engine.
4.  **Application Devs** build simple front-ends on that engine.
5.  **Content Creators** use those apps, driving content and transaction volume.
6.  This volume generates fees, attracting more **Investors** and **LYW Providers**, strengthening the entire network.

This comprehensive, six-pronged strategy ensures that IPFS Sats is built on a foundation of secure code, robust infrastructure, deep liquidity, and a compelling, user-friendly experience.
